Solution - SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It helps you optimize your websites so that they have better rankings on Search Engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. The ultimate goal of SEO is to rank higher in search engine results pages for relevant keywords and phrases, which can increase visibility, traffic, and ultimately revenue for the website. The importance of SEO - Meta tags are important for SEO because they provide information to search engines about the content on your site and can help improve your website's visibility and ranking in search engine results pages.
